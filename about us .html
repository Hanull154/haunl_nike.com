All About Nike

Who We Are

Nike, Inc. is a global leader in athletic footwear, apparel, and sports equipment. Founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, Nike has grown into a powerhouse that inspires athletes around the world. With innovative designs and cutting-edge technology, Nike empowers every individual to push their limits and perform at their best.

The company’s mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world. *If you have a body, you are an athlete.

Our Journey

Beginning as Blue Ribbon Sports, Nike officially became a stand-alone brand in 1971. Since then, it has revolutionized sportswear with landmark products like the Air Max, Nike Air, and Flyknit shoes. Nike’s iconic swoosh logo and memorable marketing campaigns, such as "Just Do It," have cemented its place in popular culture.

Innovation & Sustainability

At Nike, innovation is at our core. We invest heavily in research and development to create new materials and technologies that improve performance while reducing environmental impact.

Did you know? Nike aims to power all its facilities with 100% renewable energy by 2025.

Sustainability initiatives include recycling plastic waste into new products, reducing water usage, and minimizing waste across our supply chains.

Join the Movement

Nike's community extends beyond products; it's about empowering athletes everywhere. Whether through sports sponsorships, community programs, or inspiring campaigns, Nike is committed to fostering inclusion, diversity, and achievement.
